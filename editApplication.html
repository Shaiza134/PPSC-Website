IMPORTANT NOTE REGARDING SUBMISSION OF INCOMPLETE APPLICATION

You can only edit your application if your application has been submitted successfully (After the completion). If your application has not yet been submitted, please follow the following steps to submit your application:

  1. Visit the PPSC Website and click the "Apply Online" button from the home page.
  2. Select your post, read the instructions and enter your CNIC Number.
  3. Fill out the remaining information as required by the system and submit your application.

If your application has been submitted successfully and you have received the Application Number and Token Number for your post, enter your CNIC Number, Application Number and Token number below to edit your application.

This Token number has already been sent to your email provided in the application form. If you did not receive token number in your email inbox check Spam / Junk folder in your email account.
