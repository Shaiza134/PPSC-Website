PPSC Complaint Management System

Complaint Management System (CMS) has been introduced by the Commission in order to channelize Complaints of the Candidates. Any aggrieved candidate may file Complaint under this Complaint Management System (CMS) relating to any irregularity/illegality committed by the Officers/Officials of Punjab Public Service Commission or procedural/systematic error/omission of Punjab Public Service Commission. No information of any kind will be given through CMS. For seeking information, candidates are advised to send their questions through e-mail address ppsc@punjab.gov.pk and they may also visit PPSC website where complete information is available in "Important Instructions to Candidates" , "PPSC Planner" and "Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)" .
